# Tutorial Video Production Spec

**File:** `STIMULI/tutorial_video.mp4`  
**Purpose:** Demonstration of the click-to-place task for Phase 1 & 3.  
**Shapes:** Red square, red circle, green circle.

See `../README.md` and `../script.md` for full task documentation. The script includes ELI5/simple versions for explaining the task to participants.

The video should show **clicking to place** (not dragging). Subtitles describe what's on screen; they are not instructions read aloud.

---

## Content & Timing

| Step | Duration | Visual | Subtitle |
|------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | ~2.5 s | Three shapes on screen: red square (left), red circle (center), green circle (right) | "Three shapes appear." |
| 2 | ~1.5 s | Red square appears at center, then moves to left (simulating a click placement) | "Red square appears. Clicking to place on the left." |
| 3 | ~1.5 s | Red circle appears at center, then moves to left (grouped with square) | "Red circle appears. Clicking to place on the left." |
| 4 | ~1.5 s | Green circle appears at center, then moves to right | "Green circle appears. Clicking to place on the right." |
| 5 | ~3 s | Final layout: red shapes on left, green on right. Subtitle at bottom | "There were alternative ways of grouping, but this is what we went with." |
| 6 | ~2 s | Same layout with new subtitle | "Click to place each shape." |

**Total:** ~12–14 seconds

---

## Key Requirements

1. **Click-to-place, not drag:** Show shapes being placed by a single click at the target location. Do not show dragging.
2. **Subtitle style:** Black text, centered at bottom of screen. Describe what's happening (e.g., "Clicking to place on the left").
3. **Required subtitle:** Include exactly: "There were alternative ways of grouping, but this is what we went with."
4. **Layout:** Red square and red circle grouped on the left; green circle on the right.
5. **Resolution:** 1920×1080 recommended (matches task display).

---

## Fallback

If `tutorial_video.mp4` is missing, the task plays an animated fallback with the same sequence and subtitles.
